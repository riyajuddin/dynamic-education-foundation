About School


Dynamic Education Foundation (DEF) is a Co-Educational English Medium School. It came into existence in the year 2007. DEF was established as a Pre-Primary School (Nursery and K.G.) and Primary School (I-V) for children following the module under Board of Secondary Education Manipur (BOSEM). The School has been upgraded by a class every year till class ten (X, 2013). Now, it has classes from Pre-Nursery to Class X. DEF is run under the aegis of Public Charitable Trust- "Rural Education Foundation" bearing Register No. 250 of 2008 under Indian Trust Act. 1882, Registration Act. 1908 under Government of Manipur.
