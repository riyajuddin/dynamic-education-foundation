School Academic Section

True Education is the motto of DEF

Objectives

DEF aims at providing the best education available in some of the best schools in the state of Manipur in particular and the country as a whole in general. It aspires to provide an academic atmosphere to achieve all round development for each child and to inculcate in the children an awareness of rich cultural inheritance of Indian Society. The school has desire to achieve the A-Type Pre-Primary School (referred to as standard pre-primary schools by the Child Care Committee) especially for pre-primary standard. It also aimed to promote the high quality of Education to promote so as to improve the educational status of general public resulting into better way of life.

Procedure


PN & Nursery : First come first serve basis
K.G & Class I: Simple test for new-comer students
Class II to Class IX: Oral test for new-comer students

Seat Capacity

Class I-V : 35 children each
Class VI-X : 30 children each

Contact Address

DEF is located at the junction of four villages of Malom, Yurembam, Konthoujam and Bamdiar. It is about 9 km away from the heart of Imphal, the sate capital of Manipur.
